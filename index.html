About game


Introduction:

The game was written as my final project assignment for college. The main purpose of this game is to defeat a boss and rescue a queen. In order to do that player will take a role of chicken knight and will need to defeat many foxes on the way to final boss. This is a platform game where player can jump to other platforms in order to avoid enemy or collect "corns" with gives a special points. These corns can be exchange in the shop for health potions or extra damage points. This game is a retro style game with pixel art. It is similar to old good "Mario" games. in order to create this game I did use a visual basic language and visual studio software with the forms.Game use original content and all the animation and graphics are made by me. Overall the game got around 1300 lines of code and the size is 50 MB.


Plot:

"Long time ago in Lost World was beautiful castle ruled by Queen. Every chicken was happy in their kingdom BUT THEN on one day another fractions ruled by Master sorcerer Fox kidnapped Queen. Chicken kingdom immediately took action to retrieve their Queen from sly foxes. They send a brave Chicken Knight for this mission." - Burned Feather

As the tale above tells , the brave chicken knight grabbed his sword and went for adventure to save his beloved queen from enemy nation ruled by foxes. On the way there were many foxes which brave chicken need to defeat.

Guide:

Movments:

In order to move character during game player need to press: Right arrow to move Right. Left arrow to move Left. Up arrow to jump.

Strategy:

In order to rescue queen , player needs to defeat or avoid foxes on the way. Some of the foxes are avoidable so in order to keep health player might use this strategy but it might mean loss corns points which means player will not be able to buy many potions or attacks points. Another strategy requires to as soon as possible to buy attack points to defeat foxes on the way with only one attack and the rest of points invest in potions to survive final boss.

Shop:

Player can buy health potions or attacks points which will help him survive and defeat opponents quicker. In order to buy items player need to collect a corns during game. Choose wisely which items you would like to buy because there are limited points which you can collect.
